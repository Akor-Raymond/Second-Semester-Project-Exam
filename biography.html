Biography

Early Life

Akor Raymond's childhood was a transformative journey marked by unexpected adventures that fostered his resilient spirit and ignited his passion for success. Raised in a rural town, Raymond spent his formative years working on the family farm, cultivating grains and tubers alongside his parents and siblings.
As one of seven children – four boys and three girls – Raymond learned the value of hard work, teamwork, and perseverance from a young age. These early life experiences laid the foundation for his future accomplishments and instilled in him a strong work ethic.

Education

Akor Raymond's educational journey began at the Universal Basic Education (UBE) program, where his curiosity and passion for learning were nurtured. Throughout his time at UBE, Akor distinguished himself as a diligent and persevering student, earning recognition for his outstanding performance.
Before graduating, Akor sat for the common entrance examination and achieved the highest score in his local government area. This impressive feat secured his admission to secondary school.
Akor's academic excellence continued unabated in secondary school, where he consistently topped his class at the end of every term. He actively participated in competitions, winning numerous awards for both himself and his school.
Upon graduation, Akor successfully sat for the West African Examination Council (WAEC), National Examination Council (NECO), and Joint Admission and Matriculation Board (JAMB) examinations. His outstanding performance earned him admission to a tertiary institution.
At the university, Akor's perseverance and dedication to academic excellence continued to pay off. He graduated as one of the top students in the Biochemistry department, a testament to his hard work and determination.

Career

Upon graduating, Akor Raymond embarked on the one-year mandatory National Youth Service Corps (NYSC) program. After completing his service year, he secured a role as a Medical Sales Representative at Exus Pharmaceutical Nigeria Ltd. and was assigned to Kaduna State.
Currently, Akor Raymond works with DKT International-Nigeria, a leading social marketing organization and one of the country's largest contraceptive providers. He serves as a Medical Sales Representative, leveraging his skills and expertise to drive business growth and improve public health outcomes.
